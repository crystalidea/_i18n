Instructions for removing Mozilla Firefox and other similar Mozilla products.

  1. Download and install Uninstall Tool.
  2. Find Mozilla Firefox in the programs list (or just type "mozilla" to find all Mozilla products immediately).
  3. Double click on the found item in the list and wait while uninstall process is finished. Carefully follow Mozilla uninstaller instructions.
  4. Uninstall Tool will remove all leftover traces afterwards (using Uninstall Wizard technology).
