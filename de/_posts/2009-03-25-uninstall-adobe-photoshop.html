Instruction for removing Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Photoshop CS/CS2/CS3/CS4/CS5/CS6 and other similar Adobe products.

  1. Download and install Uninstall Tool.
  2. Find Adobe Photoshop in the programs list (you can use search filter, just type "adobe" to find immediately all Adobe Products installed on your PC).
  3. Choose Uninstall selected application or double click on the item in the list.
  4. Carefully follow all uninstaller instructions and wait while uninstall process is finished.
  5. Uninstall Tool will remove all leftover traces afterwards (using Uninstall Wizard technology).
