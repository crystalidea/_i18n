Many people dislike the new Internet Explorer 7 included in Windows Vista for its unwonted interface.

To uninstall it, you may use one the following ways:

  • The most effective way for complete uninstall of Internet Explorer 7 (or any other software) is to download and use CrystalIDEA Uninstall Tool.
  • Run this command manually:
%windir%\ie7\spuninst\spuninst.exe

Don't forget to quit all Internet Explorer windows before removal.

Many professionals recommend Mozilla Firefox as a better and more secure way of Internet surfing.
